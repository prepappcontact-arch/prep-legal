PREP

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

PREP ("the app", "we", "us") is a nutrition and meal-prep tracking application, built and maintained by an independent developer. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use PREP, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it. We have tried to write it in plain language, because you should not need a law degree to understand what happens to your data.

Who is responsible for your data

PREP is operated by its developer, reachable at prep.app.contact@gmail.com. For the purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the developer is the data controller for the personal data described below.

What we collect

Account data. When you create an account, we receive your email address and a unique account identifier. If you sign in with Google, Google provides us with your email address and basic account identifier. We do not receive your Google password or your contacts, and we do not post anything to your Google account.

Profile data you enter. PREP works by letting you enter information about yourself and the people you track: a display name, sex, age, height, weight and weight history, activity level, and nutrition goals. You choose what to enter. This data exists so the app can calculate calorie and macronutrient targets for you. That is its only purpose.

Usage data you create. Your food logs, recipes, custom food entries, favourites, shopping lists, and meal plans are stored so they are available to you across sessions and devices.

Barcode lookups. When you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts, a public non-profit food database, to retrieve product information. No account information is sent with these requests.

What we do NOT collect. PREP does not collect your location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers. The app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not sell personal data to anyone, and we do not share it with anyone except the service providers listed below, who process it on our behalf.

Where your data is stored

Your account and usage data are stored in a database operated by Supabase, our hosting provider, in the European Union (region eu-west-3, Paris, France). Some data, such as your current session and cached entries, is also stored locally on your device so the app works smoothly. This local copy is removed when you sign out or uninstall the app.

Payments

If you purchase a subscription, payment is handled entirely by Google Play. We never see or store your card details. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status; RevenueCat receives a purchase identifier, not your payment details.

Legal bases for processing

We process your data because it is necessary to provide the service you signed up for (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR). The app cannot calculate targets or keep your logs without it. Where we rely on any other basis, such as a legitimate interest in keeping the service secure (Article 6(1)(f)), we do so narrowly.

A note on health-related data: weight, dietary intake, and similar entries can be considered health data. We process these solely because you enter them for the purpose of using the app's features, and we ask for your explicit consent to do so when you create a profile (Article 9(2)(a) GDPR).

How long we keep it

Your data is kept for as long as your account exists. If you delete your account, all of it is permanently deleted: profiles, logs, weight history, recipes, and custom foods. There is no soft-delete period and no backup we quietly keep.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, receive a copy of it in a portable format, restrict or object to its processing, and withdraw consent at any time. Most of these you can exercise directly in the app: everything you have entered is visible and editable, and Settings, then Delete account, erases everything permanently. For anything else, email prep.app.contact@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In Romania this is the Autoritatea Nationala de Supraveghere a Prelucrarii Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP), www.dataprotection.ro.

Children

PREP is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that matters, we will update the date at the top and inform you in the app before the change takes effect. We will never quietly expand what we collect.

Questions about this policy or your data: prep.app.contact@gmail.com
